Fintech / Trading

TETRIS

Algorithmic Trading Engine

Overview

TETRIS is a real-time trading engine designed around one idea: clear complexity one stacked decision at a time. It watches live market data, scores it against multiple strategies, and acts within a tight latency budget — with risk management as a first-class citizen, not an afterthought.

The Challenge

Markets move continuously and don't pause for slow code. The system needed to ingest streaming data, evaluate signals in parallel, and execute decisively — all without ever risking more capital than the rules allowed.

The Approach

  • A low-latency ingestion pipeline for streaming live market data.
  • A strategy and signal engine that evaluates multiple approaches in parallel.
  • A risk management layer that enforces position and exposure limits before any order leaves the system.
  • A backtesting harness for validating strategies against historical data before they touch a live market.

Stack

  • Python
  • Node.js
  • WebSocket
  • Redis
  • PostgreSQL
  • Docker
Artificial Intelligence

FRIDAY

AI Assistant

Overview

FRIDAY is a conversational AI assistant built to do more than answer questions — it understands intent, reasons over context, and takes real action through connected tools, responding like it actually understood what was asked.

The Challenge

Most "AI assistants" stop at generating text. The goal here was an assistant that could parse a request, decide what needed to happen, and reliably carry it out — while staying coherent across a multi-turn conversation.

The Approach

  • An intent parsing and natural language understanding layer.
  • A tool-calling orchestration layer that routes requests to the right action.
  • Contextual memory so the assistant stays coherent across a conversation.
  • A response generation layer tuned for clarity over verbosity.

Stack

  • Python
  • LLM Orchestration
  • Vector Search
  • NLP
  • REST APIs
